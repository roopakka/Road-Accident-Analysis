# 🚗 Road Accident Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes road accident patterns using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**. The objective is to uncover trends in accident occurrences, severity distribution, weather conditions, and high-risk regions to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze accident trends across states and cities.
* Identify the severity distribution of accidents.
* Study the impact of weather conditions on accident risk.
* Examine hourly accident patterns.
* Create an interactive Power BI dashboard for visualization and insights.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Accidents
* Total Casualties
* Fatal Accidents
* Fatal Accident Percentage
* Average Risk Score

---

## 📈 Dashboard Visualizations

* Accidents by State
* Accident Severity Distribution
* Accidents by City
* Hourly Accident Trend
* Risk Score by Weather Condition
* Interactive Filters for State, Weather, and Severity

---

## 🔑 Key Insights

* Maharashtra recorded the highest number of accidents among all states.
* Minor accidents accounted for more than 55% of all accidents.
* Fatal accidents represented approximately 15% of total accidents.
* Foggy weather conditions exhibited the highest average risk score.
* More than 35,000 casualties were recorded across 20,000 accidents.
* Accident frequency varied throughout the day, indicating peak accident hours.
